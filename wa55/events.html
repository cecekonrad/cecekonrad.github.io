Events

Weekly Gatherings

Student Dinner every Tuesday from 6–7 pm. Always a veggie and gluten-free option. LGBTQ+ affirming. No religious demands. Just food and people.

After Dinner from 7–8 pm: Guest Speakers, intentional conversations, Bible studies, and fun activities. Conversations are always curious, expansive, and welcoming of tough questions.

Volunteer Shifts

Join us to prep, cook, and clean for a free student meal hosted every Tuesday night! Shifts are typically between 4–6 pm and 4–7:30 pm.
