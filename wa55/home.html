Bread+Belonging Tuesdays

Student Dinner 6–7 pm. Always a veggie and gluten-free option. LGBTQ+ affirming. No religious demands. Just food and people.

After Dinner 7–8 pm: Guest Speakers. Intentional Conversations. Bible Studies. Fun Stuff. Our conversations are always curious, expansive, and welcoming of tough questions.

Who We Are

Bread+Belonging is an affirming and inviting community for all students. Whether you come for dinner, worship with us, or get involved with retreats and service projects, there is a place for you.

We affirm the worth and dignity of all persons and their gender identity, race, and sexual orientation.
