Where I am headed.

These are the two main goals I am focused on right now.

Work with fashion and beauty brands

I want to be part of a creative team for a fashion or beauty company and help design ads, campaigns, and visual content. I like being involved in how a brand looks and feels across different spaces.

In the future I see myself working on art direction, layouts, and campaign ideas for brands that care about style and storytelling. I want my work to feel fresh, confident, and connected to the people it reaches.

Grow my design and coding skills

I want to keep improving in VS Code and front end basics so I can update my website and create sites for other people. Building things from scratch helps me understand my style and what I want my work to look like.

I also want to get stronger in Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and After Effects so I can create more polished projects and bring my ideas to life without feeling limited by my skills.
