What inspires me.

I like paying attention to the visuals around me, and a lot of what I notice shows up in my work. These are a few of the things that inspire me and keep me creative.

Travel

I love to travel and look at the visuals of different places. The visual identity of every place is important and you can learn a lot from different storefronts, signage, color palettes, and even packaging in other cities.

Fashion

I’ve always been into fashion, and I want to work in that space in the future. I pay attention to how brands style and present themselves because the visuals say so much without using words.

Design

I like design because it’s a big part of advertising. Layout, type, and color change how people feel about a brand, and I want to learn how to use those things in a simple and clean way.

Photography

I take a lot of photos because I like framing things and playing with color. It helps me think about mood and composition in design work too.

Cooking

I've always really enjoyed cooking but recently I've gained a new appreciation for the different styling, textures, and colors of food. There’s a lot of design in it, and I notice how food brands and restaurants can make things look appealing.
